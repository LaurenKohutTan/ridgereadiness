Ridge Readiness

Welcome Incoming Freshmen!

This is a website for you to prepare for Ridge! Using the menu bar above you can navigate different part of our websites and by clicking on the Ridge logo it will bring you back to this home page. The "Preparation" tab will give you information on what you need to do over the summer to be prepared for school. The "What to Expect" tab will tell you an estimate on what to expect from classes and how much homework each class gives according to students. The interactive map is a map that will show you Ridge and the quickest routes to class. The quiz will test you knowlege about Ridge High School so you can see if your ready for Ridge. The "Advice" tab will give you advice from current RHS students. The "About Us" tab will give you information about us, the creators of this website! Lastly, the "A Look inside Ridge" tab will include a map where you can click different location and you will be able to see pictures of these different locations so you can better know the hallways of Ridge!
